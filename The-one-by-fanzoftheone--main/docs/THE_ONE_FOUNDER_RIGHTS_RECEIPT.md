THE ONE is a philosophy and system built to help people grow.

“You Are The One” is a metaphor reminding individuals that growth begins with their own mind and actions.

Every person has strengths and weaknesses. Where one struggles another can help. Where someone excels they can teach.

THE ONE exists to help remove barriers that stop people from reaching their potential.

The goal is to support growth of:

Mind  
Body  
Soul

through cooperation, learning, creation, and shared knowledge.
